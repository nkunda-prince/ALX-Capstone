About CampusConnect

Connecting students with their perfect home away from home since 2019

Our Mission

At CampusConnect, we believe that finding the right place to live shouldn't be a barrier to education. Our mission is to make student housing accessible, affordable, and stress-free for every student pursuing their academic dreams.

We started this platform after experiencing firsthand the challenges of finding safe, affordable housing near campus. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of students across Rwanda, helping them focus on what matters most - their education.
